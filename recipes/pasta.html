Turkey Bolognese with Penne

Description

Turkey Bolognese with Penne is a hearty, flavorful dish combining lean ground turkey with a rich tomato-based sauce, served over tender penne pasta. The sauce is made by saut√©ing onions, garlic, and carrots, then simmering turkey with crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and Italian herbs for a deep, savory flavor. A splash of cream or milk adds a velvety touch. Toss the cooked penne in the sauce, garnish with fresh basil and Parmesan cheese, and serve for a comforting, protein-packed meal perfect for any weeknight.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 3/4 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ground thyme
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup tomato juice
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • 1 (8 ounce) package penne pasta
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat. Add ground turkey and cook until well browned, 5 to 7 minutes. Add carrots, onion, celery garlic, thyme, and red pepper flakes. Cook and stir until vegetables are tender, 5 to 10 minutes. Drain and discard grease, if necessary. Add white wine; cook and stir until almost completely evaporated, about 5 minutes. Add tomato sauce, tomato juice, and parsley. Reduce heat and simmer until sauce reach a thick consistency, about 20 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water ot a boil. Add penne and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 11 minutes. Drain and return to the pot. Add the sauce and Parmesan cheese; mix well. Season with salt and pepper.
